About Our AI Interview Platform

AI Mock Interview is a professional Artificial Intelligence based interview preparation platform created to help students, freshers, and job seekers improve interview performance, communication skills, confidence, technical knowledge, and placement readiness.

Smart AI-Based Preparation

This platform combines modern web technologies and Artificial Intelligence systems to create a realistic interview preparation experience with real-time performance analysis.

Why This Project Was Built?

Many students struggle during placements because they lack interview confidence, communication skills, and practical interview experience.

This project was developed to solve that problem by providing realistic AI interview simulations for HR, Technical, and Aptitude rounds.

Users can practice interviews, answer questions through voice or text, and receive smart AI-generated feedback instantly.
